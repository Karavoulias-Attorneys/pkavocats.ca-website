Welcome to
Karavoulias Attorneys

Our priority at Karavoulias Attorneys is the complete satisfaction of each of our clients. We do not hesitate to explore all solutions which are offered within the legal framework of our file. In doing so, lawyers at Karavoulias Avocats use rigor, determination, originality and innovation in the development of strategies to adopt in the management of the files entrusted to them.

We promote a strong relationship of trust with our clients, operating in complete transparency and in constant collaboration with regards to them. This allows them to be constantly aware of the evolution of their case file while playing an active role in the conduct of it. Our ultimate goal is to provide high quality legal services and to meet even the most specific needs of our clientele.

We contribute our know-how with the ultimate goal of finding a solution that will meet the expectations of our clients. Choosing Karavoulias Attorneys is choosing to take your case to heart and work closely with the lawyers of our team, who make it a point to be available to answer your questions. Indeed, we advocate superior customer service, while always putting our client's satisfaction first.

Our Legal Team:

Peter Karavoulias

Founder - Attorney

Me Karavoulias works primarily in corporate and commercial law. His field of expertise includes the areas of bankruptcy and insolvency, real estate and business law, as well as civil litigation in general. Skilled negotiator and litigator, Me Karavoulias offers his solid experience and expertise at your disposal to obtain a fully customized legal solution to all your needs.

Cynthia Laprise

Attorney

A graduate of the Université de Montréal (LL.B.), Me. Laprise mainly practices litigation, civil and commercial law. In particular, her field of practice revolves the areas of real estate, mortgage, insurance and civil law. The needs and satisfaction of her clients always being a priority, Me Laprise works with rigor and meticulousness with the sole aim of finding solutions adapted to their problems. In doing so, Me Laprise prioritizes communication with her clients so that, in close collaboration, objectives are achieved effectively.

Tessa Roy-Hébert

Attorney

Tessa Roy-Hébert joined our team as a lawyer in June 2017, after a career in civil and commercial litigation. She is currently completing her master's degree in North American common law at the Université de Montréal and is writing her dissertation on multi-jurisdictional collective actions. Her practical experience in civil and commercial litigation as well as in labor law and succession law has allowed her to develop a strong interest in advocacy and client relations.
